# FSnap - Facebook Video Downloader

FSnap is a premium browser extension designed to make downloading videos from Facebook as simple as a single click. It detects videos on your Facebook feed, Reels, and Watch pages, providing high-quality download options directly on the video player.

- **Single-Click Download**: Injects a sleek download button directly onto Facebook videos.
- **High Quality**: Automatically extracts HD and SD sources from Facebook's video metadata.
- **Reels Support**: Works seamlessly with Facebook Reels.
- **Premium UI**: Modern, glassmorphism-inspired design that matches the Facebook aesthetic.
- **Fast & Lightweight**: Minimal performance impact on your browsing experience.

## üìñ How to Use

1. Navigate to [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com).
2. Find any video you want to download.
3. Hover over the video to see the blue **FSnap Download Button** in the top right corner.
4. Click the button and select your preferred quality (HD or SD).
5. The video will start downloading immediately!
